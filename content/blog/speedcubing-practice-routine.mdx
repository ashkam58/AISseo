Want to improve your speedcubing time? Follow this practice routine to go from 60 seconds to 30 seconds.

## Warm-Up

- Spend 5 minutes solving the cube slowly.
- Focus on accuracy and finger placement.

## Drills

- Practice cross and F2L separately.
- Time yourself for each step.
- Aim for consistency.

## Timed Solves

- Do 10 timed solves daily.
- Analyze your mistakes.
- Focus on improving weak areas.

## FAQ

**Q: How long does it take to see improvement?**
A: With consistent practice, you can see results in 2–4 weeks.

**Q: Do I need a specific cube?**
A: A speedcube is recommended for better performance.
